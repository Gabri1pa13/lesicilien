What areas of Palermo have villas?

Villa rentals in Palermo are concentrated in three zones: the historic center (walkable, cultural, best for city stays), Mondello (beach village 12 km from the city, sandy beach, family-friendly), and the provincial coast (30-40 km from Palermo, direct sea access, full privacy).
