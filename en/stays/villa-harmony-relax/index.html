The Property

Absolute relaxation in Mondello, the jewel of Palermo's coast

Villa Harmony Relax is a luxury retreat in the heart of Mondello, the famous white-sand beach and turquoise water that made Palermo's coastline world-renowned. Here, relaxation isn't just a promise — it's a daily experience, marked by the sound of the waves, the golden light of the Sicilian sun and the scent of the sea.

The villa was designed for maximum comfort and privacy: bright, spacious interiors with touches of authentic Sicily, a fully-equipped kitchen and every detail curated for a flawless stay. Its proximity to Mondello beach means the sea is just a 5-minute walk away.

Mondello is just 20 minutes from Palermo's historic centre, home to some of Sicily's most celebrated UNESCO landmarks.
