The Property

Boutique suites in the heart of Palermo

Eleonora D'Aragona Home Suites is a collection of elegant suites named after Eleonora of Aragon, one of the most fascinating women in Sicilian history. Located in Palermo's historic centre, the suites blend the city's noble heritage with refined, contemporary design, offering an authentic and unforgettable stay.

Every suite has been curated down to the smallest detail: selected furnishings, premium linen, carefully designed lighting and every modern comfort for a truly regal stay. The bright, welcoming spaces strike the right balance between historic charm and functionality — ideal for couples looking for a romantic weekend and for families wanting to experience Palermo in style.

Set in the vibrant heart of the city, the location puts Palermo's main landmarks within walking distance: the Cathedral, Teatro Massimo, Quattro Canti and the historic markets of Vucciria and Ballarò.
