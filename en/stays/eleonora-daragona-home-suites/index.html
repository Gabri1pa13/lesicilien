The property

Boutique suites in the heart of Palermo

Eleonora D'Aragona Home Suites is a collection of elegant boutique suites named after Eleonora of Aragon, one of the most fascinating figures in Sicilian history. Located in Palermo's historic centre, the suites blend the city's noble heritage with contemporary, refined design — an authentic and unforgettable stay in the heart of Sicily.

Each suite is curated down to the smallest detail: selected furnishings, premium linen, studied lighting and all modern comforts for a regal stay. From Eleonora D'Aragona Home Suites you can explore Palermo's cathedral, Teatro Massimo, Quattro Canti and historic markets on foot.
