The Property

Your private terrace in the heart of Sicily

Fior di Sicilia is an elegant apartment featuring an exclusive private terrace, perfect for those who want to experience the most authentic Palermo bathed in Mediterranean light and scent. The property combines refined interiors with a unique outdoor space for breakfasts, sunset aperitivos and evenings under the Sicilian stars.

Every detail of the apartment has been carefully curated: refined furnishings, a fully-equipped kitchen and every modern comfort blend seamlessly with Sicilian charm. The terrace — the true highlight of the property — offers an extraordinary living experience, letting you take in the city from above, immersed in the quiet beauty of Palermo's architecture.

The central location provides easy access to Palermo's main monuments, historic markets and best restaurants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Fior di Sicilia – FAQ

Are pets allowed at Fior di Sicilia?
Pets are generally not allowed in order to preserve the fine furnishings, but please contact us to discuss specific requests.
Is parking available near the apartment?
The apartment is located in a central restricted traffic zone (ZTL). On-street parking is available outside the ZTL, or you can use nearby partner private garages.
How far is Fior di Sicilia from the main sights?
Fior di Sicilia is in the heart of the historic centre. The Cathedral, Teatro Massimo and Quattro Canti are all within a 10-minute walk.
Is the Wi-Fi connection suitable for remote work?
Yes, Fior di Sicilia has high-speed fibre Wi-Fi (up to 1Gbps), ideal for video calls, streaming and remote work.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Check-in is from 3:00 PM, and check-out is by 10:00 AM. We offer luggage storage and, where possible, flexible timing.
