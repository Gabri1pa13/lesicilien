The property

A villa by the sea, where sky meets the Mediterranean

Tilde Sunset Bay Villa is an exclusive villa with direct sea access, nestled on the stunning Palermo coastline. Here time slows to the rhythm of the waves, and the Sicilian light paints every sunset in red and gold, offering scenes of extraordinary beauty from the comfort of your private villa.

The property has been designed for maximum comfort and privacy, with large windows framing the bay and open spaces that blend into the marine landscape.

Ideal for families, groups of friends or couples seeking an authentic luxury experience. Strategically located, a short distance from Palermo and its cultural attractions, yet offering the tranquillity and natural beauty that only the Sicilian sea can provide.
