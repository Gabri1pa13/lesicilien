The Property

A villa on the sea, where the sky meets the Mediterranean

Tilde Sunset Bay Villa is an exclusive property with direct sea access, set along the beauty of the Palermo coastline. Here time slows to the rhythm of the waves, and the Sicilian light paints every sunset red and gold, offering breathtaking views right from the comfort of your private villa.

The property was designed to offer maximum comfort and privacy, with large windows framing the bay and open spaces that blend seamlessly with the seaside landscape.

Perfect for families, groups of friends or couples seeking an authentic luxury experience. The villa enjoys a strategic location, a short distance from Palermo and its cultural attractions, while still offering the peace and natural beauty that only the Sicilian sea can provide.
