Frequently Asked Questions

Villa Harmony Relax in Mondello is ideal for families — it offers a private garden, proximity to the beach and a calm residential setting. For larger families, Tilde Sunset Bay Villa provides even more space with 6 bedrooms and direct seafront access.

Which property has a sea view in Palermo?

Tilde Sunset Bay Villa offers panoramic sea views from an exclusive waterfront position. Villa Harmony Relax is located near Mondello beach with coastal surroundings. The historic centre properties are inland but within easy reach of the coast.

What is the cheapest luxury rental in Palermo?

Fior di Sicilia starts from €85 per night — the most affordable entry point into Le Sicilien's curated collection. It offers a private rooftop terrace in the historic centre, making it exceptional value for a luxury stay in Palermo.

Eleonora D'Aragona Home Suites is perfect for couples — regal suites near the Cathedral with an intimate atmosphere. Fior di Sicilia is another excellent option, offering a private rooftop terrace for romantic evenings in the heart of the city.

Can I book a private villa for a large group in Palermo?

Yes. Tilde Sunset Bay Villa accommodates up to 12 guests across 6 bedrooms, making it ideal for large groups, family reunions or celebrations. It is an exclusive waterfront estate with full concierge service.
