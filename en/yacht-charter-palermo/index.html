Yacht Charter Palermo

Private excursions, gourmet dinners on board and sunset aperitivos on the Gulf of Palermo. Every experience is arranged by our concierge, tailored to your group.

The Gulf of Palermo is one of the most beautiful stretches of coastline in the Mediterranean — framed by Monte Pellegrino to the north, open towards the Egadi Islands to the west. A private yacht charter gives you access to this landscape on your own terms: no fixed schedule, no crowds, just sea and Sicilian light.

Experiences We Arrange

Our concierge team coordinates every detail — vessel, skipper, catering and on-board chef — so you can focus entirely on the experience.

Exclusive experience

Gourmet Dinner with Private Chef

A selected chef comes on board with the finest products from Sicilian markets. Sea starters, handmade pasta, fresh catch of the day and traditional desserts — served at the table as the sun sets over the gulf. Wine pairing available on request.

Romantic experience

Sunset Aperitivo

Departure from Palermo harbour in the late afternoon, heading towards Mondello and Capo Gallo as the light shifts. Prosecco, Sicilian nibbles and a view few ever forget. The ideal excursion for couples and small groups.

Full day

Egadi Islands Excursion

Favignana, Levanzo, Marettimo — 30–40 nautical miles from Palermo. Crystal lagoons, isolated bays, snorkelling in protected waters. On-board catering included, stops at the most beautiful coves in the archipelago.

Nature & sea

Capo Gallo & Marine Reserve

Just 30 minutes from Palermo, Capo Gallo offers sea caves, lava rock seabeds and crystal-clear water up to 15 metres deep. Anchorages impossible to reach by land, accessible only by private boat.

Adventure & discovery

Ustica — Volcanic Island

52 km north of Palermo, a fully protected marine reserve. Underwater visibility up to 40 metres on calm days. A destination that deserves the full day, with flexible anchoring and return times.

FAQ — Yacht Charter Palermo

Do I need a boat licence to charter a yacht?

No. All Le Sicilien charters include a licensed professional skipper. You simply enjoy the experience.

Can I have a private chef on board the yacht?

Yes. It is one of our most requested experiences. The chef cooks on board using fresh Sicilian ingredients — sea starters, pasta, fresh fish and dessert. Everything is included in the concierge coordination.

How long does a yacht charter from Palermo last?

We offer half-day excursions (4–5 hours), full days (8–10 hours) and multi-day itineraries to the Egadi Islands, Ustica or Lipari. Every duration is customisable.

What is the best time of year for a charter in Palermo?

May to October. July and August are the hottest months. May, June and September often offer the best conditions: light wind, calm sea and fewer crowds.

What destinations can I reach by private yacht from Palermo?

From Palermo you can reach Mondello bay, Capo Gallo, the Egadi Islands (Favignana, Levanzo, Marettimo) and Ustica. Coastal excursions along the Gulf of Palermo are ideal for half-day trips.
