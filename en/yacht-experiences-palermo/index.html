Nature & sea

Capo Gallo & Marine Reserve

Thirty minutes' sailing from the port of Palermo, Capo Gallo is a strict nature reserve inaccessible by land. Sea caves, sheer limestone walls, seabeds of posidonia and protected marine life.

Perfect for half a day, morning or afternoon. Can be combined with snorkelling at Isola delle Femmine or a stop in Mondello.

Duration4–5 hours
DepartureMorning or afternoon
Group2–10 guests
Included
  • Skipper with reserve access permit
  • Snorkelling equipment
  • Drinks and light snacks on board

Adventure & discovery

Ustica — Volcanic Island

Fifty-two kilometres north of Palermo, Ustica is Italy's first fully protected marine reserve. Underwater visibility of up to 40 metres on calm days. Submerged caves, vertical walls, historic tuna fisheries.

The excursion requires a full day and favourable weather. The concierge monitors sea conditions and, if needed, proposes alternatives of equal standard.

Duration10–12 hours
DepartureBefore dawn
Group2–10 guests
Included
  • Skipper experienced on this route
  • Full catering (breakfast, lunch, snacks)
  • Snorkelling and surface-diving equipment
  • Weather monitoring and flexible return

FAQ — Experiences on Board

How long does a gourmet dinner on board a yacht last?

Dinner on board typically lasts 3–4 hours, starting in the late afternoon. The chef comes on board with fresh market ingredients and cooks directly on the boat as you sail out to sea.

Can I choose the destination for the excursion?

Yes. The concierge designs the itinerary around your preferences: half a day at Capo Gallo, a full day at the Egadi Islands or Ustica, or a completely customised route.

Are the experiences suitable for families with children?

Absolutely. Private charters adapt to every kind of group. The skipper knows the safest routes and can suggest itineraries ideal for children — calm waters, sheltered bays, shallow snorkelling.

What happens if the weather does not allow the trip?

The concierge monitors the forecast and lets you know in good time. In case of bad weather, we propose an alternative of equal standard or move the experience to another date at no charge.
