At-Sea Experiences in Palermo

Private gourmet dinner with a personal chef, sunset aperitivo on the Gulf, day cruises to the Egadi Islands — every outing is arranged in full by our concierge team.

The Gulf of Palermo is one of the most dramatically beautiful stretches of the Mediterranean. Limestone headlands dropping sheer into the sea, hidden coves that sunlight only reaches in the early morning, and a quality of light at sunset that drew painters from across Europe for centuries. Le Sicilien turns this setting into private, bespoke experiences on the water — for the people who know that the real Sicily is found between the shore and the horizon.

Every experience is entirely private. No shared boats, no fixed schedules, no standard packages. Our concierge team arranges every detail around your preferences.

Not a restaurant on the water. Your private table, on the sea.

Sunset Aperitivo

Departure from Palermo harbour in the late afternoon, heading west to follow the light. Champagne or Sicilian sparkling wine, charcuterie and cheese boards as the sky shifts from gold to amber. A simple thing, made extraordinary by the setting. Perfect for couples, birthdays, or the ideal way to begin an evening in Sicily.

Included Champagne or sparkling wine · Sicilian antipasti boards · Skipper · 2–3 hours' sailing · Concierge coordination
Day Cruise

Private Cruise to the Egadi Islands

Favignana, Levanzo, Marettimo — three islands, three entirely different characters. Transparent lagoons where the seabed is visible at 15 metres, coves reachable only by sea, a quietness that no resort can buy. Morning departure from Palermo, full day on the water with lunch on board, return at sunset.

Included Full-day sailing · Lunch on board · Snorkelling gear · Expert skipper · Stops at the best anchorages

FAQ — Charter Experiences Palermo

How long does a gourmet dinner on a yacht last?

Typically 3 to 4 hours. Departure is at sunset, sailing west along the coast. Dinner is served on deck as the sun goes down. Return to port is usually around 10 to 11pm.

How many people can join a private charter experience?

Available vessels accommodate from 2 to 12 guests depending on the experience. For larger groups we can coordinate multiple boats. All experiences are entirely private.

Are experiences available year-round?

The ideal season runs from May to October. July and August are the most popular months. Spring and early autumn often offer excellent conditions with fewer crowds — contact us for availability.

Is sailing experience required?

No. Every outing includes a licensed professional skipper who handles all navigation. You are guests, not crew. No nautical experience needed.

Can the menu be personalised?

Absolutely. The menu is agreed before departure. Any dietary preference, allergy or specific request will be accommodated by our chef.
