Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to book directly without Airbnb?

Yes. All payments are processed through Kross Travel, a certified European booking platform with secure payment processing, data protection compliance and booking confirmation guarantees. You receive a confirmed reservation with full documentation, just as you would through any major platform.

How much do I save by booking direct?

Typically 10-15%. Airbnb charges guests a service fee of 12-18% on top of the nightly rate. When you book direct with Le Sicilien, there are no platform fees — the price you see is the price you pay. On a one-week stay, this can mean savings of EUR 150-400.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) as well as bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through Kross Travel, our certified booking platform.

What happens if I need to cancel?

Our cancellation policy is flexible and discussed directly with you at the time of booking. Unlike rigid platform policies, we work with you to find a solution — whether that means rescheduling your dates or providing a credit for a future stay. Every situation is handled personally.

Is Kross Travel a legitimate booking platform?

Yes. Kross Travel is a certified European booking and property management platform used by professional hospitality operators across Italy. It provides secure payment processing, automated booking confirmations and is fully compliant with EU data protection regulations.

Can I still leave a review if I book direct?

Absolutely. After your stay, we invite you to leave a review on Google, TripAdvisor or directly on our website. Many of our direct booking guests also share their experience on social media — we are always grateful for honest feedback.
