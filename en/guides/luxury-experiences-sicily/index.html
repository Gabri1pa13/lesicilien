Luxury Experiences by Type

On the Water

The sea around Palermo offers access to some of the finest waters in the Mediterranean. Le Sicilien arranges private boat charter for coastal excursions from Palermo harbour — half-day trips to Capo Gallo and Mondello, full-day Egadi Islands itineraries, or the crossing to Ustica for diving and snorkelling in a protected marine reserve. Multi-day island itineraries can extend to the Aeolian Islands to the east.

In the Kitchen

Sicilian cuisine is one of the great regional traditions in Italy. The cooking class format — market visit in the morning, kitchen session in the afternoon, lunch or dinner together — is one of the most requested experiences among Le Sicilien's guests. It works equally well as a single session or as a recurring activity across a longer stay.

For guests who want the result without the cooking, a private chef service delivers the same quality at your villa table — with full menu customisation and wine pairing on request.

Through History

Sicily has one of the most stratified archaeological and architectural histories in the world. A private guide unlocks this in ways that a self-guided visit cannot — providing context, narrative and access to areas that are restricted or obscure. Our recommended itinerary for a historical day: morning at the Valley of the Temples (Agrigento), lunch in a local trattoria, afternoon at the Villa Romana del Casale (the finest Roman mosaic floor in existence), return via the Madonie mountains.

Through Wine

Sicily is one of Europe's most exciting wine regions. The island produces exceptional indigenous varieties — Nero d'Avola, Nerello Mascalese, Carricante, Grillo, Catarratto — that have attracted serious attention from wine critics. Le Sicilien can arrange visits to producers in the Etna DOC area, the Marsala wine zone, or the volcanic island of Pantelleria for its extraordinary Zibibbo passito wine.
